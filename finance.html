Objectives

To facilitate the implementation of inclusive growth and equity and allocate resources judiciously.
To ensure fiscal discipline in the spending of public money by all the state government departments.
To prepare detailed plans and budgetary allocations as per the policy instruments envisioned by the state government.
To prepare, implement and review medium term financial roadmap ensuring fiscal stability and progressive approach.
To provide transparent and proactive governance to the people of Gujarat by acting as a guardian of the state exchequer.
To be an instrument of all-round development of Gujarat.


Vision and Mission

To facilitate development and inclusive growth for Gujarat through prudent and progressive Financial Management.
Improve State's Financial Management System continuously, and position Gujarat as a benchmark in Financial Discipline, Fiscal Management, etc.
To ensure responsive and citizen oriented systems to serve people as well as the direct stakeholders such as tax dealers, employees, pensioners etc.
To develop efficient and transparent system for enabling financial transactions/ functions of different Government bodies using technological interventions
